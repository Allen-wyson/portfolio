Challenges & Solutions

Balancing structural rigidity with weight was the central engineering challenge. Initial designs exceeded the target mass by 8%. Iterative topology refinement — removing material from low-stress regions identified by FEA — and switching two non-critical members to thinner wall thickness brought the frame within budget without compromising torsional stiffness. Manufacturing jig design was co-developed to ensure weld joint angles were achievable on the team's equipment.
