About

Yo-Shiun Cheng

I am a Mechanical Engineering Master's student at EPFL with a strong focus on structural design, dynamic control, CAE analysis, and robotics. My approach to engineering is hands-on, problem-driven, and fast under pressure — the kind built from leading a group in Formula Student team, designing race car spaceframes, and building soft robots that speak.

From FEA simulations at Delta Electronics to autonomous robot research at NTU, I bridge the gap between simulation and physical systems. Currently, I am developing a soft robotic vocal tract at CREATE Lab, EPFL.
